About

I am a Research Scientist at RebuilderAI in Seongnam, South Korea. I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from KAIST under the supervision of Prof. Minhyuk Sung. I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at KAIST in 2022.

My current research interests lie in machine learning for 3D Computer Vision and Graphics (Deformation, Generation, and Editing), and I am also broadly interested in Neural Radiance Fields, Diffusion Model and Multimodal Understanding.

For details, refer to my Curriculum Vitae (CV).
